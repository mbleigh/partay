Play Phraseology

(super-mega-alpha version)

How to Play

Phraseology is a team-based guessing game for 4-16 players. A deck of phrases is constructed with each player seeing part of the deck in advance. Players take turns trying to get their teams to guess as many phrases as possible in one minute until all phrases have been guessed.

Play takes place in three rounds, with the same deck used in each round. Pay close attention, you'll need to remember what phrases are out there to succeed in the later rounds.

In Round One, players can give any kind of clues they'd like - anything except the words of the phrase itself!

In Round Two, players can only give a single word as the clue for each phrase.

In Round Three, players must be silent and type out emoji as the only clue for each phrase.

The team with the most guessed phrases across all rounds after round three is the winner!
